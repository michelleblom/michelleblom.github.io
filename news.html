News

July, 2024

Our work on improving the computational efficiency of AWAIRE post-election audits has been accepted to EVOTE-ID’24 in Tarragona, Spain!

July, 2024

Honoured to have received the 2023 FEIT Excellence Award for Research for my work on risk limiting audits.

October, 2023

Honoured to have received the 2023 IEEE Cybersecurity Award for Practice alongside Vanessa Teague, Philip Stark, and Mark Lindeman.

April, 2023

Our work on RLAs for two seat STV elections was presented at the VOTING’23 workshop in the Carribbean! This work presents improvements to our original methods, presented two years prior, allowing for more two seat STV elections to be audited and with fewer required ballot samples.

February 10, 2022

Our work on risk limiting audits for Ranked Pairs elections has been accepted to the VOTING’23 workshop. Joint work with Damjan Vukcevic, Peter Stuckey, and Vanessa Teague.

September 30, 2022

Our work on using Dirichlet-Tree models for ballot-polling audits of IRV elections is being presented today at the first International Workshop on Election Infrastructure Security (EIS’22).

October 3-6, 2022

It’s time for the 8th International Joint Conference on Electronic Voting (EVOTE-ID’22)! Our team will be presenting work on initial steps toward using Dirichlet-Tree models for IRV post-election audits.

May 6, 2022

Our work on risk limiting audits for 2-seat STV elections will be presented today at VOTING’22!

January 1, 2022

This past year our team has explored risk-limiting audit methodologies for both party-list proportional and Hamiltonian elections. We have also been successful in our ARC Discovery Project application on post-election audits for Single Transferable Vote (STV) elections!

December 24, 2021

In an early Christmas present, our team has been awarded an ARC Discovery Project grant. This project will focus on developing post-election audit methodologies for Single Transferable Vote (STV) elections.

January 1, 2021

Despite the COVID-19 pandemic sending us Melbournians home for the majority of the year in 2020, this past year has been a busy one on the election auditing and analysis front. We presented three papers at EVOTE-ID’20, winning a best paper award for ‘You can do RLAs for IRV’ summarising the first pilot of our methodology for risk-limiting audits of IRV elections. Early in the year, we presented work at IAAI on exploring whether certain mistakes, such as lost ballot boxes, could have had caused specific candidates to lose a seat in the context of STV elections.
